Lake Worth, FL

Location

Lake Worth, FL is located roughly 60 miles north of Miami.

About

Weather

It's warm pretty much all year

Population

Roughly 40,000 people call Lake Worth their home

Sports

Baseball, Volleyball, Football, and Basketball are played year-round here.

Things To Do

  • Visit the beach
  • Go fishing
  • Go to high-end upscale shopping outlets and malls
